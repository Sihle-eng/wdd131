Site Name: MCHOBOKAZI REAL ESTATE

Site Purpose

This website is a real estate listing platform that showcases houses for sale. It provides users with detailed information about various properties, including images, prices, locations, and features. The platform aims to connect potential buyers with sellers and facilitate the home buying process.

  • Showcase a list of available homes with photos, prices and key details
  • Inlcude contact forms for inquiries and scheduling viewings
  • Optional blog or tips section for buyers and sellers
  • Mortgage calculator where buyers input figures to estimate monthly payments, total interest and affordability range

Scenarios

  • "Can I filter listings to only show houses within my budget and preferred neighbothood" As a potential homebuyer, I want to filter listings based on my budget and preferred neighborhood so that I can easily find houses that meet my criteria.
  • "Does the house have a virtual tour or photos I can view before scheduling a visit" As a user, I want to see virtual tours or photos of the houses listed on the platform so that I can get a better idea of the property's condition and layout before scheduling an in-person visit.

Color Schema

The site will use a clean and professional color palette to create a trustworthy and appealing look.

  • Primary Color - Deep Slate Blue (#2c3e50):For headings, navigation bar and call-to-action buttons.
  • Accent Color - Soft Coral (#e74c3c):Used for highlights, and price tags to draw attention.
  • Background - Light Gray (#ecf0f1):Used for page background to maintain readability.
  • Text Color - Dark Charcoal (#34495e):Used for body paragrapahs and descriptions.

Typography

The site will use a combination of fonts to ensure readability and a modern look.

  • Heading font - "Montserrat", sans-serif: A bold and modern font for headings and titles to create a strong visual hierarchy.
  • Body font - "Open Sans", sans-serif: A clean and highly readable font for body text and descriptions to ensure clarity.
  • Accent font - "Roboto", sans-serif:For quotes or featured listings.
